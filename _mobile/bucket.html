Description

Cash is better with Bucket. We are solving the problem of physical coins by digitising them at the point of sale. When you shop with cash in participating retail locations, tell them to “Bucket The Change” and any coins due back to you are digitised and you are given a Bucket receipt. Use the Bucket app to enter or scan the QR Code and YAY! Your loose change is now safe and secure in your account. Once your account reaches $50, you can cash out to a prepaid credit card, gift card or donate to a charity.

Main Features
  • Save your money instead of letting it sit in your coin jar at home.
  • Give back to your community. Bucket makes it easy to save and then spend or donate.
  • Scan easily with our native QR Code scanner.
