Description

A 2D game created using Microsoft XNA for one of our final requirements in our AI class. It’s a simple time killing app. You control the car using the arrow keys on the keyboard to surpass all the other vehicles to gain points. Your car’s speed increases as the time runs. In order for you to survive longer, you have to do your best to catch the power-up to slow your car down. This game has a hi-score functionality that makes it even more challenging. When you hit the other cars, you lose the game and get to see if you have beaten the hi-score. You could retry the game and play as long as you like.

Technologies
